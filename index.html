✦ How it works

Three ways to get reminded

GeoToDo covers every scenario — whether you need a location trigger, a calendar alert, or both at once.

Location

Alert fires the moment you step near any grocery store, pharmacy, or any place you choose — even with your phone locked.

"Remind me to buy milk at any grocery store"

Date & Time

Schedule a one-time alert or set a recurring weekly schedule. Works like an alarm — fires exactly when you need it.

"Remind me every Monday at 9 AM"

Location + Time

The geofence activates on a specific date and fires when you arrive. A fallback notification fires on that date even if you never reach the place.

"Remind me Thursday — if I'm near the pharmacy"

✦ Features

Everything you need to
never forget again

GeoToDo works silently in the background, using adaptive GPS polling to save battery while staying sharp.

Specific Place Reminders

Pin any address or business. GeoToDo alerts you the moment you arrive — even if you forgot you set it.

Category Triggers

Set a reminder for "any grocery store" or "any pharmacy" — GeoToDo finds them near you automatically using Google Places.

Date & Time Alerts

One-time or weekly recurring time-based reminders — a full calendar alarm inside your to-do app.

Built-in Checklists

Add checklist items to any reminder. Check them off right from the alert — no need to open the app.

Recurring Reminders

Set a cooldown period and GeoToDo re-alerts you on every visit, every week — whatever cadence you need.

Smart Snooze

Not the right moment? Snooze any alert for minutes, hours, or days. It resurfaces right on schedule.

Custom Categories

Create your own location groups — regular spots, favourite coffee shops, work sites — with manual pins or Google search.

Drives Through DND

Alerts use a high-priority channel that can bypass Android driving mode and iOS Focus — so you never miss a reminder while in the car.
