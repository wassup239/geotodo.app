✦ Features

Everything you need to
never forget again

GeoToDo works silently in the background and fires the moment you step into a saved location.

Specific Place Reminders

Pin any address or point of interest. GeoToDo alerts you the moment you arrive — even if you forgot you set it.

Category Triggers

Set a reminder for "any grocery store" or "any pharmacy" and GeoToDo finds them near you automatically.

Built-in Checklists

Add checklist items to any reminder. Check them off right from the alert — no need to open the app.

Recurring Reminders

Some reminders need to fire every time you visit. Set a cooldown period and GeoToDo handles the rest.

Smart Snooze

Not the right moment? Snooze any alert for hours or days. It will resurface right on schedule.

Custom Categories

Create your own location categories — your regular spots, favourite coffee shops, or work sites.

Your data stays on your device

GeoToDo stores all your reminders and location data locally. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. Location is processed entirely on-device — we never see where you are.
